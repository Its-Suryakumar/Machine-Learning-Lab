Model Intepretation¶

Why did we use Simple Linear Regression?¶

Simple Linear Regression is used to model a relationship between two continuous variables. Linear Regression describes the correlation between an independent and a possible dependent varibale. Here our dataset has 1 dependent and independent features and hence it will be the best suit.¶

Model Observation¶

  • Coefficient i.e, the Slope of the line is 9360.2613, which means the change of Y for a unit of increase in X.
  • The intercept represents the value of the dependent variable when all independent variables are zero. In the case of a salary vs. experience linear regression model here, the intercept of 26777.39 would represent the estimated starting salary for individuals with zero years of experience..
